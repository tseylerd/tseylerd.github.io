About the app

Motion Beats simulates car engine sounds using your phone’s built‑in motion sensors. It adapts the sound in real time to your speed and acceleration — no external hardware required.

Choose from a selection of unique engine sounds: from classic gasoline and diesel to futuristic synthetic and spaceship-style effects. One sound is available for free, others can be unlocked all at once.

All processing happens on your device. No accounts. No analytics. Just sound.
