XSpeak

Your private AI Meeting Companion. Fully on-device.

XSpeak is a fully private AI meeting companion for Mac and iPad. Once set up, it transcribes your meetings from speech and uses on-device AI to analyze the conversation and provide personalized insights — all without sending data to the cloud.

  • Fast and accurate real-time transcription
  • Private and offline — all processing happens on your device
  • Insights tailored to you — XSpeak distinguishes your voice in meetings

Getting Started

  1. Install XSpeak from the App Store
  2. Open the app
  3. Complete initial setup following the instructions:
    • Download AI model (~5GB)
    • Create voice profile (requires microphone access)
    • Choose a subscription plan
  4. Tap Start Meeting Recording to begin your session
  5. To receive AI-generated summaries and insights, tap AI Actions button and choose AI action:
    • Full Analysis
    • Summary
    • Insights
    • Suggestion
