Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

XSpeak is a privacy-first AI meeting assistant. We are committed to protecting your privacy by ensuring that all your data stays on your device. This Privacy Policy explains how XSpeak handles your information.

2. No Data Collection

  • We do not collect your name, email, contacts, or any personal identifiers.
  • We do not use third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs.
  • We do not connect to external servers for processing or storage.

3. On-Device Processing

All core features of XSpeak run entirely on your device:

  • Transcription of meetings is performed in real-time using on-device audio processing.
  • AI analysis (summarization and insights) is powered by an on-device language model.
  • If you create a voice profile, it is stored locally in the form of anonymized embeddings and remains on the device as long as the app is installed.

4. Temporary Recording Storage

XSpeak temporarily stores a short segment of the meeting audio on your device to enable real-time transcription and analysis:

  • Only the last 300 seconds (5 minutes) of audio are kept in memory at any time.
  • Audio is stored locally and is not saved permanently.
  • All audio is automatically deleted when:
    • the app is closed,
    • the Reset button is tapped,
    • or new audio overwrites the previous buffer.
  • Recordings are never uploaded, transmitted, or stored externally.

5. User-Initiated AI

AI analysis is performed only when manually triggered by the user. XSpeak does not perform any automatic or background analysis.

6. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how XSpeak handles your data, you can contact us through the support link provided in the App Store listing.
